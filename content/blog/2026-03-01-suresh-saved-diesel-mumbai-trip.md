# Suresh saved ₹2,000 in diesel on one Mumbai trip. Here's how.

Route AI doesn't just shave minutes — it picks the cheapest path. Real numbers from a 3-day haul.

## The route

Suresh was hauling steel from Mumbai to Jaipur — a 1,150 km journey. His usual route: Mumbai → Nashik → Indore → Jaipur.

## The old way

- Distance: 1,180 km
- Diesel cost: ₹18,500
- Toll charges: ₹2,200
- Total: ₹20,700

## The AI suggestion

TranZfort's Route AI suggested: Mumbai → Surat → Vadodara → Udaipur → Jaipur

- Distance: 1,150 km (30 km shorter)
- Diesel cost: ₹16,500
- Toll charges: ₹1,800
- Total: ₹18,300

## The savings

- ₹2,400 less diesel
- ₹400 less in tolls
- **Total saved: ₹2,800**

Suresh now uses Route AI for every trip. The savings add up to over ₹80,000 per year per truck.
