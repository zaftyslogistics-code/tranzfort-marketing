# Hindi voice goes GA: lessons from 3 months of beta

What we learned shipping native Hindi TTS and STT to thousands of drivers — the wins, the bugs, and the surprises.

## The beta journey

We launched Hindi voice in beta with 500 drivers across 5 states. The goal: test if native Hindi text-to-speech and speech-to-text would actually be used on the road.

## What worked

- Drivers loved speaking in Hindi instead of typing
- Voice commands were 3x faster than manual entry
- Offline mode was critical — many areas have no data

## What surprised us

- 40% of voice queries were in mixed Hindi-English (Hinglish)
- Background noise was a bigger challenge than expected
- Regional accents varied more than we anticipated

## How we fixed it

- Retrained the model on Hinglish patterns
- Added noise suppression at the audio level
- Expanded training data with regional dialects

Hindi voice is now GA and working for thousands of drivers across India.
