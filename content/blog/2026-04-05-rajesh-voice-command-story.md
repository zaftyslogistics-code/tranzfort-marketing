# From 12 calls to 1 voice command: Rajesh's day, before and after

Meet Rajesh Kumar, a fleet owner from Jaipur, who replaced his broker rolodex with a single 'find loads' command.

## Before TranZfort

Rajesh's mornings started with 12+ phone calls to brokers, checking rates, negotiating terms, and hoping for a good load. By 11 AM, he'd spent more time on calls than driving.

## After TranZfort

Now, Rajesh just says "find loads to Delhi" in Hindi. The AI assistant shows him available loads, rates, and truck requirements. One tap to confirm, and he's on the road.

## The impact

- 2 hours saved per day
- Better rates (no broker commission)
- More loads, more earnings

Rajesh now manages 8 trucks instead of 4, all because he got his time back.
